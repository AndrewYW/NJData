NJData Overview
Overview

NJData is a project covering New Jersey's presidential election voting history from 2004 to 2016. While New Jersey has been historically Democratic, a variety of factors may influence voting patterns, including gender, GDP, population growth, etc. We've taken a few of those and made some graphs and tables for you to view.
